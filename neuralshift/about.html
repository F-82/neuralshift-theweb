Most Sri Lankan businesses run on instinct, WhatsApp groups, and overworked spreadsheets. We think that can change.

Our Story

NeuralShift was founded by a team that spent years watching smart, hardworking business owners struggle with the same avoidable problems: orders falling through the cracks, inventory nobody trusted, customer data living in someone’s phone.

The tools existed. The technology existed. But nothing was designed specifically for the way businesses here actually operate — with small teams, high pressure, and owners wearing five hats at once.

So we built it ourselves. A system powerful enough to handle real operational complexity, but simple enough that anyone on your team can use it from day one.

Our Mission

Bring structure and intelligence to how businesses operate. Not just for large enterprises — but for the restaurant owner with three outlets, the café that’s growing too fast to manage manually, the cloud kitchen that needs to scale without losing control.

Operational clarity is the foundation of growth. When you can see clearly, you can move fast.
