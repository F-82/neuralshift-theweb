Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 June 2026

NeuralShift is a marketing website. This page explains, in plain language, what information the site handles and what it does not. We have kept it short because the site itself does very little with your data.

The contact form

If you choose to fill in our contact form, it collects your name, business name, phone number, industry, and your message. We only receive these details when you decide to submit the form — nothing is collected just by visiting the site.

This information is not stored on any NeuralShift server or database. When you submit the form, it opens WhatsApp with your details pre-filled, and you send the message to us yourself. That means your details go directly into WhatsApp's messaging service and are handled under WhatsApp and Meta's own privacy terms.

Analytics and cookies

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand, in aggregate, how people use the site — which pages are visited and roughly where visitors come from. Google Analytics sets cookies in your browser to do this. The data we see is anonymous and aggregated; we do not use it to identify individual visitors.

Getting in touch about your data

If you have any questions about this policy or about information you have sent us over WhatsApp, message us on WhatsApp or use our contact page.
